Lemonade Recipe

Description:

Whether its hot or cold, everybody loves a nice chilly lemonade!

Ingredients:

  • 6 lemons, juiced
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup ice cubes

Steps:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine fresh lemon juice and water. Stir in brown sugar until it dissolves. Stir in ice cubes.
